About us

Banka is a light-weight core banking application that powers banking operation on the go, which aims to ease banking operations for its users. Ever wanted to do operations like create an account or check account transactions, and you don't want to visit any of our branch offices? Banka App helps you do all of that, saving you time and money.

What we offer

1

Create Bank Account

Creating a Banka account online is secured and it takes you less than 2 minutes to do so.

2

Monitor Transactions

Our online platform gives you the ability to monitor all your day to day account transaction. No more having to wait or queue before getting your account breakdown.

3

Pay Utility Bills

With Banka app, you can pay all your utility bills from your finger tips: PHCN Bills, Water Bills, DSTV bills and much more.
